EduGrids - Education Website Template

EduGrids is a free and premium education HTML template that is crafted for all types of education web portal such as - university, online course websites, LMS, college, school and training institutes. This template is designed to provide you with the best possible user-experience. Its minimal, modern and refreshing design will impress anyone. EduGrids comes with 20+ essential education site specific pages, 3 unique homepage variations and every single element & sections you need to build a complete education website. This highly customizable education web template is coded in block-based structure, well-documented and delivered with Sass files, so you can extensively customize it depending on your needs. If you are looking for modern & great looking HTML template that built-with Bootstrap 5 and comes with all essential page and elements that need for a education website then, EduGrids is perfect choice for you.
